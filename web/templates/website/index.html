Welcome to Empire

The Empire of Valon has existed for six hundred years. The imperial house exerts only loose control over its major vassals and the empire much resembles a broad confederation or alliance. Nonetheless, for most, that has meant six centuries of relative peace after the cataclysmic Third Greytide. The empire's neighbours have rarely been able to challenge it openly. Some worry that times are changing.

The city of Sanctuary is the capital of the empire and home to the imperial court, a term encapsulating any noble who happens to be in the city, and any commoner able to move in noble circles. Sanctuary is the social heart of the empire, the place where alliances are formed and plans made, where even the most restless of adventurers go to find companions and to plan their next steps.

Empire is a multiplayer text-based roleplaying game. In it, you portray a character from the empire. The day-to-day of the game is focused on the city of Sanctuary, but you can take actions and participate in events which reach far beyond it.

For additional information about the game and its setting, see the world information pages.

Empire uses a character roster system. This means that all characters are already written and you can choose one to play. You can find a complete list on the characters page.

Before applying for a character, please read the rules.
